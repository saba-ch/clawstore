# Macro Calculation Rules

## Calorie density per gram

- Protein: 4 calories per gram
- Carbohydrates: 4 calories per gram
- Fat: 9 calories per gram
- Alcohol: 7 calories per gram

## Estimation guidelines

When the user describes a meal without exact portions:

- "a chicken salad" → assume 150g chicken breast, 2 cups mixed greens, 1 tbsp dressing → ~300 cal
- "a bowl of rice" → assume 1.5 cups cooked → ~310 cal
- "a sandwich" → assume 2 slices bread, 3oz deli meat, 1 slice cheese, condiments → ~400 cal
- "a handful of nuts" → assume 1 oz → ~165 cal

When in doubt, estimate slightly high rather than low. It's better to overcount than undercount for tracking purposes.
